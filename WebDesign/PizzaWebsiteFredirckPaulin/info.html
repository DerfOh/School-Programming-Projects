Hungry Student pizza shop - Info

The most delicious pies in town.

An enterprising young student, Bob decided that many college students like to have pizza delivered to their dorms for late night study halls and all night parties. The student decided to open up Hungry Student Pizza Shop , which specializes in all night delivery to the local college campuses in the Flint area.

Bob and his staff provide a variety of pizza styles to both students and the different college offices and staff.

Customers may pay by major credit card, cash, check, or approved vouchers from the college
